## 4. Strategic context — re-state for next session

### 4.1 Who's our actual peer

zkID does **not** target passports. Their roadmap is EUDI alignment around
SD-JWT + mDL. Our scope (ICAO 9303 + SD-JWT + X.509 + mDL) is broader on
issuer formats but narrower in academic positioning.

Treat zkID as a **sibling**, not a parent or downstream. We can borrow their
predicate evaluator design, mDoc circuits, and bench methodology, but we
shouldn't pretend to match feature-for-feature on their EUDI generic-AC core.

### 4.2 Where we lead

Our 4 extensions (E spec) are real lead points:

1. **Path A** — `pk_digest` folded into Pedersen commitment so device binding
   becomes a linking property
2. **composite_show** — multi-credential bundle under one ECDSA
3. **CSCA Master List Merkle inclusion** — trust root proven in-circuit
4. **DSC revocation SMT** — non-membership proven in-circuit (zkID puts
   revocation explicitly out-of-paper)

These are publishable. If we want academic citation, write up a short paper
based on `spec/passport-ac-extensions.md`.

### 4.3 Where we lag

1. **Predicate flexibility** (deferred Direction B)
2. **Mobile evidence** (no on-device numbers; zkID published theirs)
3. **mDoc support** (zkID just shipped, we're behind — Direction D)
4. **Reblind / prepareBatch** (paper has it, our backend doesn't expose
   reblind primitives so we re-prove every session)
